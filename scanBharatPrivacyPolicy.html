Privacy Policy

Scan Bharat (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates the Scan Bharat – Document Scanner mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or location.

b) Document & Image Data

All scanned documents, images, PDFs, and files are stored only on your device. We do not upload, store, or share your files on any server.

c) Camera Permission

Camera access is required to scan documents. Camera data is used only for scanning purposes and is never shared.

d) Storage Permission

Storage access is used to save scanned files on your device. We do not access files unrelated to the app.

Data Security

Since Scan Bharat does not store data on servers, your documents remain safe on your device.

Children’s Privacy

Scan Bharat does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.
